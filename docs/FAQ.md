# Frequently Asked Questions — MaxSpeed VPN

### General

**Q: What is MaxSpeed VPN?**
A: MaxSpeed VPN is a fast, secure virtual-private-network app for
Android and iOS. It encrypts your internet traffic and routes it
through global servers, protecting your privacy and unlocking
geo-restricted content.

**Q: Is MaxSpeed VPN free?**
A: Yes. A free tier is available with a selection of servers.
Premium subscriptions unlock all servers, higher speeds, and
adaptive split-tunnelling.

---

### Account & Subscription

**Q: How do I subscribe?**
A: Open the app → tap the crown icon → choose Monthly or Yearly.
Payments are processed through the Google Play Store or Apple App
Store (no credit-card data touches our servers).

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing
period. Manage subscriptions in your platform's store settings.

**Q: Does my subscription carry over if I switch phones?**
A: Absolutely. Sign in with the same account and restore purchases
from the Settings screen.

---

### Connection

**Q: Why is my connection slow?**
A: Common causes:
  • You're connected to a geographically distant server.
  • Network congestion during peak hours.
  • Your ISP is throttling VPN traffic.
Try switching to a nearby server or enabling "Quick Connect,"
which auto-selects the fastest available node.

**Q: Why does the VPN disconnect randomly?**
A: On Android, aggressive battery optimisation can kill the
foreground service. Go to Settings → MaxSpeed VPN → Battery → set
to "Unrestricted" to prevent this.

**Q: Can I use MaxSpeed VPN on multiple devices?**
A: A single premium account supports up to 5 simultaneous
connections.

---

### Privacy & Security

**Q: Do you keep logs of my activity?**
A: No. MaxSpeed VPN operates a strict no-logs policy. We only
store anonymised diagnostic data (cancelled after 30 days) to
improve server performance.

**Q: What encryption do you use?**
A: WireGuard®-style tunnels with ChaCha20-Poly1305 encryption and
Curve25519 key exchange. TLS 1.3 is used for the control channel.

**Q: Is it safe for banking and shopping?**
A: Yes. Your traffic is fully encrypted end-to-end. No
man-in-the-middle interception is possible.

---

### Features

**Q: What is split tunnelling?**
A: It lets you choose which apps go through the VPN and which use
your regular connection locally. Useful for banking apps that block
VPNs while keeping the rest of your traffic encrypted.

**Q: Does MaxSpeed VPN work with streaming services?**
A: We continuously rotate IPs to stay ahead of blocks, but no VPN
can guarantee 100 % success. Premium servers are optimised for
streaming; look for the play-icon badge.

**Q: Is there a kill switch?**
A: Yes. Enable "Always-On VPN" in Settings. If the tunnel drops,
all network traffic is blocked until the connection is restored.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: The app crashes on launch.**
A: Please update to the latest version. If the issue persists,
clear the app cache (Android: Settings → Apps → MaxSpeed VPN →
Clear Cache) and restart.

**Q: I see "Authentication failed."**
A: Your session may have expired. Sign out and sign back in, or
check that your subscription is still active.

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Email support@maxspeedvpn.com or use the in-app chat (tap the
question-mark icon). Average response time: under 4 hours.
